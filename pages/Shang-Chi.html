This 2021 American superhero film based on Marvel Comics features the character Shang-Chi. In the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU), it is the 25th film produced by Marvel Studios and distributed by Walt Disney Studios Motion Pictures.

When Shang-Chi and his sister Xialing (Zhang) are drawn into a search for a mythical village, by their father Wenwu (Leung), the leader of the Ten Rings organization, he is forced to confront his past.
